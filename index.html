Jack & Jones Wicker Hat with Drawstring - Beige

This wicker hat from Jack & Jones, embellished with a drawstring, is the perfect accessory to complete a summer look. Its versatile beige hue and wicker construction make it ideal for sunny days, offering style and protection from the sun.
